About JDP

Jared Davis Photography was born out a pure love for christ and photography. Belonging to Triumph Chruch with over 80 ministries, I was charged by my Pastor to become active. When I joined the photography ministry, I became intimate with my camera and the ministry lead taught me a great deal. Pretty soon I was being asked if I shot professionally? Therefore JDP was born. God has truly blessed me with opportunities all over the nation and I look forward to capturing memorable moments for you.
